Facts

'In 15 years from today, I'll be home, standing on red sand,
looking up at the clear sky, looking for a little pale blue dot,
looking for our planet earth.'
In 2033, humanity will start its first journey beyond what is known. Onboard will be the first humans to hopefully take their first step onto the red planet, Mars.
Planting the trees for future generations, propelling society to the next level.

First renders of BFR early 2016 - Courtesy of SpaceX

What will you've achieved by then? What are your dreams you are working so hard on? For most of us, space travel and settling on Mars is nothing more than rocket-science, pun intended. But what Alyssa Carson, callsign BLUEBERRY, said on her plenary speech at BBYO IC 2018 made me realize that there are people out there, who understand the importance of space travel, people who are like me, who understand me.

Starship on Mars base alpha - Courtesy of SpaceX

Almost like a secret door had open up that always had been there but was hidden behind what some parts of conventional society screw up. Have you ever wondered, what it would feel like to stand on the surface of Mars, looking for planet earth?
How it would be possible to create a society on a planet so far away from our origins.
I did. And many others as well. We are a new society, whose goal it is to settle on a new planet.
Because we are a community, we are Generation Space!
