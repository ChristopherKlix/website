About
Me

Hi, my name is Chris. As you probably just clicked on 'About me' or you just scrolled here by accident and started reading, I guess you are interested in my person. And I appreciate that!

I am 25 years young and I live on the beautiful planet Earth. Right next to Alpha Centauri, Earth is part of an 8 planet solar system with one sun that will be around for about another 5 billion years and then turn into a red giant. I moved here because of the variety in society that can be found in this place. But also the appreciation of innovative and new tech made me realize that this is my planet, my home.

But I recently heard about Proxima Centauri b. Very interesting...

A few weeks ago, I started a new podcast called 'Generation Space'. I am very interested in tech and physics, so when everybody else is watching the Super Bowl, I am watching the livestream of SpaceX showing their latest launch of incredible human engineering talent. Others might call it Falcon 9, Heavy or our recent accomplishment the Starship (BFR).
Oh, and go Rams!!!

Based on some subtle hints on this site you might have also figured out that I am a bit into Computer Science. Actually, I am London's #1 agent for Smart Homes.
